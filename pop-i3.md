# i3wm-ez
A script that installs i3-gaps on your Linux Machine.

## **Installation Steps**
<br>

### STEP 1

Open Terminal

### STEP 2

    cd Downloads

### STEP 3

    git clone https://github.com/iamdevnitesh/i3wm-ez

### STEP 4

    cd i3wm-ez

### STEP 5

    sudo chmod +x popOS.sh

### STEP 6

    ./popOS.sh

<br>
<br>

### **REBOOT INTO YOUR PC**

Select i3 on the login screen

<br>
<br>
